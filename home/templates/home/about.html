To make the students in the awareness of god’s presence within themselves through spiritual guidance, meditation, reflection and prayer on various occasion and the daily morning assembly

To create and sustain a conducive teaching- learning atmosphere for the mastery of knowledge, through regular evaluation & supervision of classroom – Instruction, seminars, refresher courses and input session for teachers, introducing technology and new approaches needed for effective classroom – instruction

To develop positive attitude and skills needed for school life through value education classes, Guidance and counseling, social service, Help the needy programme etc. with emphasis on respect for human life, dignity of labor, justice, love peace and concern for the poor.

To promote integration among students belonging to different, religions and social groups through value education classes, Celebration of feasts, the thereby promoting in the students acceptance of cultural difference

To provide wholesome activities that foster love for the country and appreciation and enrichment of the Indian culture and heritage through cultural and various house –wise competitions, celebration of national feasts, Morning assembly , craft exhibitions etc.

To prepare students for responsible leadership and selfless services through NSS activities, social services programmers, student council and other responsibilities.

To promote physical and emotional health through consistent improvement to physical plan. Sports Activities, Co-curricular activities and personal guidance

To promote care for environment by planting tree, gardening, use of eco-friendly materials, conservation of water and electricity and taking good care of the common property
